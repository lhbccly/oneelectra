Global Charging Standard
We Support

1. Type 1 (SAEJ1772)

The standard for North America and Japan, a single-phase AC connector ideal for home, workplace, and public Level1/Level2 charging up to ~19kW.

2. Type 2 (IEC 62196-2 / Mennekes)

The primary standard across Europe, UK, Australia, and Most international markets, supporting both single-and three-phase AC charging up to 22kW and compatible with DC fast charging systems.

3. National Standard (GB/T)

China's official domestic standard for AC and DC charging. widely used in China and for Chinese-manufactured EVs and infrastructure projects globally.

4. Tesla / NACS

Tesla's unified North America charging Standard (SAEJ3400)-now open for broader adoption-supports both AC and DC charging in one compact design, with regional compatibility adapters included.
